About Us

Welcome to Closet.AI. Our website allows you to upload images of your clothes and create stylish outfits in seconds!

  1. Upload Your Items: Navigate to the "Generate Outfits" page and use the "Choose Shirts / Choose Pants" option to upload pictures of your shirts, t-shirts, pants.
  2. Image Processing: Our website will enhance your images, making them look presentable.
  3. Generate Outfits: Our AI will create stylish outfit combinations using your uploaded items.
  4. View & Download: View your generated outfits and click the "Download" button to save your favorite combinations.
  5. Explore More: Upload new items and try different combinations to find your perfect style!

This website is a realization of a broader vision: Although the current version focuses on shirts and pants, future updates will integrate AI for more responsive, efficient, and optimized results. Our ultimate goal is to expand beyond just clothing items, offering a platform that can generate complete outfits with a wide range of fashion elements. Stay tuned for more exciting features!
